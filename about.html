Our Story

Founded in 2010, Robert Walter Recruitment Agency has grown from a small Canadian firm into a global talent powerhouse. We specialize in connecting skilled professionals with leading organizations across North America, Europe, and Africa.

With headquarters in Canada and offices spanning 19 countries, we bring a truly international perspective to recruitment. Our team of 200+ recruitment specialists understands local markets while leveraging our global network to find the perfect match.
